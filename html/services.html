DUPLEX

A duplex is a modern and practical solution for suburban living, designed to fit on standard blocks of land. It consists of two separate dwellings under the same roof, each with its own entrance, amenities, garage, and often an alfresco area. Despite being underrated, the duplex offers versatile options in the property market. You can choose to live in one dwelling and rent out the other, or rent out both dwellings for rental income. If separate titles are available, you can even sell one or both of the dwellings, or move your family in next door for close-knit living.

For many, a duplex presents a great investment opportunity, with potential for rental income or resale value. It also serves as a practical solution for multigenerational families, offering separate living spaces within close proximity. With its flexibility and functionality, a duplex is a modern and convenient choice for those seeking innovative housing options.

ACREAGE HOMES

Rural living offers a plethora of advantages. The neighbourhood is peaceful and serene, with the gentle rustling of the wind through nearby gum trees providing a soothing soundtrack. You can revel in the luxury of breathing in fresh, clean air and basking in the sunshine without the constant noise of passing cars.

Furthermore, the ample space that comes with rural living allows you to build a stunning home where your family can thrive and grow. Acreage home designs are a perfect match for those who dream of tranquil rural living. These designs offer a wide range of benefits, such as large windows that capture panoramic views, outdoor entertaining areas for enjoying the natural surroundings, and much more. With an acreage home design, you can truly optimize the advantages of rural living and create a haven that is tailored to your lifestyle and preferences.
